FortiGate 7.4 Administration Study Guide, page 115 (Firewall Policy - Service)

If you don't add DNS to the policy, you just get a timeout and the browser cannot find the site. Once you add DNS, you get a prompt that you must log in to access the internet. The confusing part of this question is that it reads as if the user is able to access the internet and is not being prompted. In fact, they are not getting prompted AND they can't access the internet.
