Why would the firewall policy not block a well-known virus, for example EICAR?





Explanation:

Without deep inspection, you would never find a virus in HTTPS traffic. You will only catch a virus when it is send to you via HTTP or FTP with these settings

ANTI-V USE DEEP INSPECTION TO ENSURE FULL CONTENT INSPECTIONS PREFORMED

References:

  • Fortinet Documentation - Deep Inspection

Correct Answer:
D. The firewall policy does not apply deep content inspection.
