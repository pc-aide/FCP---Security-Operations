Question 16

What is the primary FortiGate election process when the HA override setting is disabled?




Explanation:

When the HA override setting is disabled, FortiGate's primary election process follows the order of: the number of connected monitored ports (M), followed by HA uptime (U), then Priority (P), and finally the FortiGate serial number (S).

References:

  • Fortinet Community - HA Primary Unit Selection Process

Correct Answer:
D. Connected monitored ports > HA uptime > Priority > FortiGate serial number
