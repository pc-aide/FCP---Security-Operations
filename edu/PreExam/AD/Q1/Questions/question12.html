Question 12

An employee needs to connect to the office through a high-latency internet connection. Which SSL VPN setting should the administrator adjust to prevent SSL VPN negotiation failure?




Explanation:

This setting controls the timeout for the DTLS hello packet. If the connection has high latency, adjusting this timeout allows more time for the handshake, preventing negotiation failures.

References:

  • SSL VPN Timers Explanation - Fortinet Community

Correct Answer:
C. SSL VPN dtls-hello-timeout
