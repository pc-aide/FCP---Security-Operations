Question 11

Refer to the exhibits.

Based on the configuration, what will happen to Apple FaceTime if there are only a few calls originating or incoming?




Explanation:

It says "if there are only a few calls originating or incoming?" and additionally the name of the filter is "excessive bandwidth

IN this case we only need to take into account the position of the filter, the application, and the action. Fortigate will analyze the filter from top to bottom like a regular firewall policy. Therefore we have position, then facetime and then action block the final result will be "Block face time"
